Local-first by design

The forge runs in your browser. Imported cards, avatars, and drafts are handled locally unless you choose to export and upload them somewhere yourself.

What can it export?

The editor currently supports Character Card V2 JSON and PNG cards with embedded card metadata. Lorebook editing is being built into the same forge system.
