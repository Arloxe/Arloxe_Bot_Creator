Privacy

Your cards stay in your browser.

Arloxe Card Forge is designed as a static, browser-based tool. It does not require an account or upload your cards to a server.

Local processing

Files you import are processed in your browser. The site reads your selected JSON or PNG card file locally so it can display and edit the card data.

Local storage

The editor may save draft data in your browser using local storage. This allows your current draft to remain available after refreshing the page.

Local storage stays on your device/browser unless you clear it, switch browsers, or use browser tools that sync local site data.

No backend storage

The GitHub Pages version of this site does not include a backend database. It does not store uploaded cards, avatars, or lorebooks on a server.

External links

Links to third-party websites, such as GitHub or compatible card platforms, are governed by those websites' own privacy policies.

Clearing your data

You can clear saved drafts by clearing this site's browser storage through your browser settings.
